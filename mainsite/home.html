FAQ

How can I use Hannibot?
You can easily invite Hannibot with just a few clicks. Click the 'Add Bot' button and follow the steps to invite it to your server.
I encountered an error while using the bot. What should I do?
Hannibot has a feature called /bugreport. You can submit the error code through this feature, and we will resolve it as soon as possible.
What languages does Hannibot support?
Currently supported languages are Korean and English. We plan to support more languages in the future.
